Superb Pineapple Beer

[https://www.hulettssugar.co.za/step_into_our_kitchen_superb_pineapple_beer_thirst_quenchers_recipes#]

Makes 6 litres

# Ingredients
- Peel of 1 Pineapple
- 6 litres Lukewarm Water
- 750ml (3 cups) Huletts White Sugar
- 125ml ( ½ cup) Chopped Raisins
- 10g (1 packet) Dried Yeast

# Method
1. Make sure the peel is really clean. Scrub it very well, then rinse thoroughly. Cut up the peel roughly.
1. Mix the peel, water, sugar and raisins in a large container. Sprinkle the yeast over the surface and allow to stand for 30 minutes.
1. Stir well and leave for 24 hours.
1. Strain and bottle. Put the caps on the bottles after 12 hours.
1. Store in a cool place.
