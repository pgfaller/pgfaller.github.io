Lockdown: Homemade pineapple beer

[https://www.thesouthafrican.com/lifestyle/food/recipes/lockdown-make-your-own-homemade-pineapple-beer-using-three-ingredients/]

# Ingredients for pineapple beer
3 pineapples
1 kg sugar
Water

# Method
1. Peel all three pineapples and cut them up. Do not throw away the peels. Cut the peels into small pieces and combine with the pineapple pieces. Place the pineapple pieces and peel into a 9ℓ bucket.
1. Add the sugar.
1. Fill the bucket to the top with water.
1. Use a muslin cloth to cover the top.
1. Let it stand for about two days until you can see froth.

## Beer-making tips
Adding more sugar will speed up the process. Leaving it to stand for longer makes the beer stronger. When you taste the beer and are happy with the kick, it means it’s ready.

Strain the beer through cloth in order to separate the liquid from the thicker pieces of pineapple pulp.

Bottle your beer. Leave the beer out of the fridge to ferment for longer, if you wish. When stored in a fridge, it will last up to 30 days.
