Variance and Standard Deviation

Variance is a summary statistic intended to describe the variability or spread of a distribution. The variance of a set of values $x_i$ with [Mean](../Statistics/Mean.md) $\bar{x}$ is:
$$S^2=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i} (x_i - \bar{x})^2$$
The square root of Variance, S, is the standard deviation.
